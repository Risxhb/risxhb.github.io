Development update

Dungeon Journal, Party Follow, And Floor One Field Lessons

Latest homepage notes for the Eve playable build.

Dungeon journal work now gives the build a proper in-game reference for Moonroot Hollow, including floor entries, traps, monsters, NPCs, and the guild forward base on floor one. The party solo-to-follow movement pass is also in, so the lead character can travel while the rest of the group follows in formation.

The playable dungeon path now has two working authored levels: floor one as a guild training base and floor two as the first live dungeon descent. NPC quest hooks and dialogue menus have been wired through the village, tutorial, shops, floor-one guild characters, and the descent guard so the current build feels more like a real RPG loop instead of a static map tour.

  • Dungeon journal entries for floors, traps, monsters, and floor-one NPCs.
  • Visible party leader and follower movement for the field party.
  • Two authored Moonroot levels with traps, loot, encounters, and descent flow.
  • NPC quest starts, shop options, dialogue menus, and objective progression.
