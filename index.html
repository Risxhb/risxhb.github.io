Command doctrine

You are not above the battle.
You are inside it.

Take the helm of a heavy carrier while issuing orders to persistent wings and capital escorts. Fight command strikes, interceptions, defenses, escorts, captures, and extractions in one uninterrupted battlespace.

01

Fly the flagship

Command a textured 120-meter carrier with heavy persistent throttle, double-click vector flight, a unified mouse view, placed flak screens, guided salvos, one nuclear torpedo, automated defenses, and layered durability.

02

Direct the fleet

Open a live holographic map without pausing. Place flak from the tactical overlay, lock contacts through the interactive overview, and issue approach, orbit, or keep-distance geometry while your carrier holds course.

03

Own the flight cycle

Launch fighters and drones through mirrored side bays. Manage ammunition and endurance, protect recovery lanes, service survivors, and queue physical post-service redeployment.

Fleet archive

Assemble your battlegroup.

Ships are authored tactical sidegrades—not randomized-stat loot. Layered armor, faction hull plating, bridge windows, turrets, engines, class-specific geometry, engine trails, and progressive battle damage make every ship readable in the command network.
